Government of Northern Islands

Permanent Resident (PR) Card

Your PR Card is official proof of permanent resident status in the Northern Islands. It’s required for travel and re-entry by commercial carriers.

What is a PR Card?

The PR Card is a wallet-sized document that confirms your permanent resident status. It’s essential for re-entry into the Northern Islands via air, sea, or land.

Apply for Your First PR Card

  1. Gather ID and proof of PR status
  2. Get a compliant photo
  3. Complete the application forms
  4. Pay the fees
  5. Submit your application package

Renew an Expiring Card

Renew your PR Card about 6 months before expiry, especially if you plan to travel. You’ll need updated documents and a new photo.

Replace a Lost or Damaged Card

If your PR Card is lost, stolen, or damaged, report it immediately and submit a replacement application with new photos and ID.
